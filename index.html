Biography

I received my Ph.D. in Computer and Information Systems from the University of Aizu, Japan in March 2022. Since April 2022, I have been working as an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Hainan University.
My main research interests include Machine Learning, Signal Processing, and Seamless Physiological Monitoring, with specific focus on ECG signal processing and applications, personal identification, and health monitoring.
